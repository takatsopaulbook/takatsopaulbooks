About the Book

TSHENOLO YA MONASARE: Mantswe a Makgotshwane is a powerful and spiritual reflection on society, our faith, and our collective responsibility to stand against the forces that seek to divide and harm. Takatso Paul Mokoena addresses the devastating impact of bullying, calling on every member of society to rise up with courage, compassion, and the strength of divine purpose. Through this book, readers will not only be enlightened by scriptural truths but also empowered to live in faith, love, and community.

About the Author

Takatso Paul Mokoena is a South African author with a heart full of purpose and a passion for writing about the truth and the word of God. His vision is to inspire individuals, especially the youth, to walk in the light of faith, stand against injustice, and find strength in their relationship with the Almighty. He believes in the importance of using literature to uplift souls and transform lives. Takatso Paul is committed to sharing messages that bring hope, healing, and spiritual understanding.
